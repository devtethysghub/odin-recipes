Ice Gola

Ice gola is an Indian quick to make cooling dessert, highly popular amongst kids.

It is made by crushing ice into a snow-like consistency and then shaping it into a cone or ball. The crushed ice is then drizzled with colorful and flavorful syrups, such as rose, khus, orange, or lemon, to give it a sweet and tangy taste. The vibrant colors of the syrups make the ice gola visually appealing and enticing to eat.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups crushed ice
  • 1/2 cup syrup (rose, cola, orange, or lemon)
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (almonds, cashews)

Preparation:

  1. Crush the ice into a snow-like consistency.
  2. Shape the crushed ice into a cone or ball by putting it into a small glass or cup.
  3. Drizzle with syrup and garnish with chopped nuts as per your taste preference.
